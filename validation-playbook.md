# Circle Week 1-2 Manual Validation Playbook

## Goal

Prove that people in Madhapur, Kondapur, and Gachibowli will join small offline activities and actually show up.

Do not optimize for app downloads yet. Optimize for attendance, repeat attendance, and safety.

## Week 1 Setup

1. Publish the landing page.
2. Create a WhatsApp community with separate groups per activity.
3. Create an Instagram page.
4. Recruit 5-10 trusted hosts.
5. Confirm 3 cafe/restaurant partners and 1 outdoor meeting point.
6. Run 6 manually hosted activities.

## Week 2 Setup

1. Repeat the best 3 activities from week 1.
2. Add 3 new activities based on demand.
3. Ask every attendee for feedback within 2 hours after the meetup.
4. Invite repeat attendees to host the next activity.
5. Decide whether the MVP app should start with all 6 activity types or only the top 3.

## Seed Activities

### New to Hyderabad Coffee Circle

- Best venue: cafe in Madhapur or Kondapur
- Best time: weekday 6:30 PM or Sunday 11:00 AM
- Ideal audience: freshers, migrants, new employees, students
- Host job: introductions, simple icebreaker, keep it non-awkward

### Madhapur Board Games Circle

- Best venue: board-game-friendly cafe
- Best time: Saturday 5:00 PM
- Ideal audience: introverts, students, remote workers
- Host job: pick simple games, avoid complex rules for first-timers

### Gachibowli Sunday Run Circle

- Best venue: public park or open public route
- Best time: Sunday 6:30 AM
- Ideal audience: beginners, fitness users, working professionals
- Host job: keep pace beginner-friendly, end with chai/breakfast

### Kondapur Freshers Dinner Circle

- Best venue: casual restaurant
- Best time: Friday or Saturday 7:30 PM
- Ideal audience: new joiners, people in PGs, college students
- Host job: reserve table, keep group capped at 8-10

### Remote Workers Work & Coffee Circle

- Best venue: coworking space or work-friendly cafe
- Best time: weekday 4:00 PM to 6:30 PM
- Ideal audience: remote workers, freelancers, founders
- Host job: focused work first, short conversation after

### Startup Founders Chai Circle

- Best venue: cafe near HITEC City, T-Hub area, or coworking space
- Best time: Wednesday 6:30 PM
- Ideal audience: founders, operators, builders
- Host job: no pitching pressure, one clear conversation theme

## Venue Outreach Script

Hi, we are launching Circle, a Hyderabad-first community app for small safe meetups.

We want to bring 6-10 verified people to your cafe during non-peak hours for coffee, board games, or founder meetups.

We do not need a discount to start. We only need permission to reserve one table for 90 minutes. We will list your venue as a Circle-friendly location and tag you on Instagram.

Can we run one small pilot meetup this week?

## Host Rules

- Meet only in public venues.
- Keep every activity capped at 10 people.
- Start and end on time.
- Take attendance.
- Watch for uncomfortable behavior.
- Report safety issues immediately.
- Do not allow dating-style behavior or pressure.
- Do not move the group to a private location.

## Metrics To Track

Use `validation-tracker.csv` for every activity.

Important numbers:

- Registered count
- WhatsApp joined count
- Showed up count
- Repeat attendee count
- Gender ratio
- Safety complaints
- Host rating
- Venue rating

Strong signal:

- 30% or more of registered users show up
- 25% or more of attendees come again
- Hosts are willing to host again
- Venues are willing to reserve tables again
- Women attendees report feeling safe

Weak signal:

- People join WhatsApp but do not attend
- Attendees say it felt awkward or unsafe
- Hosts need too much manual effort
- Venues dislike the group
- One activity type gets all demand and others fail

## First MVP Decision

After 2 weeks, build the app around the top 3 activity types only.

Recommended app MVP if validation works:

- Phone OTP
- Profile
- Activity feed
- Join/leave
- Group chat after joining
- Host controls
- Reports and blocks
- Admin approval
